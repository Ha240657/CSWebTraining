Module 1: Password Security

Learn how to create and manage strong passwords to prevent unauthorised access to your accounts.

What is Password Security?

Password security is the practice of making strong and complex passwords to protect personal and confidential information from cyber-attacks. It is crucial in cybersecurity to prevent unauthorized access, identity theft and data breaches.

How to Create a Strong Password

To make a strong password it is crucial to have a password mixed with symbols, numbers and letters which are lower case and upper case to avoid predictable patterns. You can also use password managers to store and generate your password for your many accounts.

What You Should Avoid When Creating Passwords

When creating a password, it is recommended that you do not contain any content that can be easily guessed, such as names, birthdays, and common words. It is also recommended that all your accounts have different passwords so that if one account is ever hacked, the others will be safe for the time being. Also, avoid sharing passwords with others. By avoiding these mistakes, your account and data will be protected from possible threats and attacks.

Take the password “johnsmith”. It is very weak, and it is easy to gain access to any account with that password.
