Overview

This training provides essential knowledge and skills to protect yourself from cyber threats. Learn how to create strong passwords, avoid phishing attacks, and use devices and networks safely.

Module 1: Password Security

Learn how to create and manage strong passwords to prevent unauthorised access to your accounts.

Module 2: Phishing Awareness

Understand how phishing attacks work and how to spot suspicious emails and messages.

Module 3: Device & Network Safety

Learn best practices for securing your devices and safely connecting to networks, especially public Wi-Fi.

Module 4: Social Engineering

Explore tactics cybercriminals use to manipulate individuals into divulging confidential information.

Module 5: Secure Remote Work

Get tips for staying secure while working from home or on the go, including VPN usage and device encryption.
